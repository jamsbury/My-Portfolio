the project

You have hired STS DESIGN to complete a specific project or task, as agreed upon in your proposal of services document. The proposed cost of the project only includes the services outlined within that document, and nothing more. If you would like us to expand on your project with additional services or features or otherwise expand the scope, additional costs will be involved. Minor adjustments to existing content, layout, color, and/or functionality that is completed before the final sign off of the project are included and will not incur any additional costs.

Making major changes (changing the scope of a project) early in the project will cost you less. The actual cost will depend on the project and the intensity of the changes.

Each unsigned Proposal of Services Document is valid for 30 days after it is received. If the document has not been approved within that time period, there will need to be a new proposal.

revisions

Revisions are the amount of major adjustments we will make to a project prior to the sign-off. For instance, a meeting over a draft of your website that leads to a set of layout or functionality changes would qualify as a revision.

Your proposal of services document will state how many additional revisions your project or milestone is allowed. Any revisions that are requested past those detailed in the proposal of service document count as a change of scope for the project and will incur a change order fee based on the type of changes.

time frame

The time frame of your project is outlined in your proposal of services document. The time frames given are rough estimates based on our previous experience, current workload, the complexity of the project, and material availability. It also assumes that our communication is consistent and any process documents are approved within 5 business days.

We keep an active queue of work and do not begin work on a project until the initial payment has been received. We make it our goal to start each project as soon as possible, and we will not begin work on a project until we can give it our full attention.

archival & continuation fees

Delayment typically arises during review periods, while we wait on content, materials, approvals, or payments. If it takes more than two weeks for us to hear back from the client, the project will be considered delayed. By delaying a project, we temporarily halt all work until contact is restored with the client.

If a project is delayed for more than half of its estimated time frame (small projects) or half of the time frame of the milestone (large projects) we will archive it and require a continuation fee to place it back in our queue.

The continuation fee of a project will be 15% of the total cost outlined in the Proposal of Services Document, up to a cap of $400 USD.

payments and milestones

The exact terms of payment are outlined in your proposal of services document. Typically, we require a 50% down-payment in order for us to place a project in our queue of work. In the case of larger projects, we might collect payments at specific milestones laid out in the proposal of services document. Once our team reaches a milestone your project will not move forward until we receive the payment to move forward. These milestone payments act as a sign-off to the completed milestone.

Our milestones are ordered so that the most important material is completed first. If you request changes to milestone 1 materials while we are working on milestone 3, you will incur a change order fee. Please ensure that you are confident with the work you receive before giving us the final sign-off on it.

meetings

We will attempt to schedule meetings with you at various points throughout your project. The amount of meetings will depend on the length and complexity of the project. Meetings will typically be scheduled via email or by phone.

If you require your meeting to be rescheduled, we will be flexible. However, please provide us with at least 24 hours notice if you require us to reschedule a meeting. Should you miss a meeting or provide us with notice on the same day you may be subject to a missed meeting fee that correlates with our hourly rate of $32 USD.

refunds and cancellations

If you wish to cancel the project after this agreement and the proposal of services have been executed, you will be refunded the deposit of the project minus the cost of any time that our team has spent on the project. For instance, if we spent 10 hours on the development of a website those 10 hours would not be refundable upon cancellation.

Upon the cancellation of the project, we will cease work and you will not be held to any future milestone payments.

If you wish to cancel the project and remove your website from our hosting, we require a 30 days notice to remove your information from our server and close out your account. Additional transfer fees may apply to transfer your hosting services. You will still pay the monthly hosting fee for the final month, and after the 30 days and completed hosting transfer, there will be no additional charges added to your account.

changes after launch

We are happy to help you with changes to your site after launch! However, any changes or modifications that were not included in the original proposal of service document will incur our hourly rate for completion. Hourly rate is $32 USD.

If you are utilizing one of the continued support plans - which are outlined in your proposal of services document - you will receive those hours of work per month for adjustments, changes, bug fixes, and expansions. These hours are non-transferable and expire at the end of each billing cycle.

In either case, work that expands the scope of the original project document, such as adding additional functionality, will require a new services document and will be treated as an additional project. This also applies to changes and adjustments that will take our team more than your support plan’s allotted hours to complete.

intellectual property

You will own the content that STS DESIGN, LLC creates as they are displayed on your website, social media, prints, or other documents. If you need the high resolution versions of the images or the working files, additional licensing may be required. We will work with you to decide the pricing and terms of further licensing based on the scope of the project.

For the purposes of this agreement, working files means RAW images, Adobe Photoshop files, Adobe Illustrator files, Adobe InDesign files, Adobe After Effects files, Adobe Premiere files, and uncompressed web images.

You own your websites and are free to take the files and host them on your own services if requested.

When we utilize images or other content that we did not create (such as stock photos), it has been licensed for use on your site or web app by a third party. That third party will retain ownership of the content in question according to their own terms and conditions. In the case of a stock photo, we may pay for the license to use the photo on your website but neither of us own the image. If you have additional questions about licensing- please ask.

During the process of creating a website, we will create snippets of code and functionality. This work is used on your web site, but we retain ownership of these as our intellectual property so that we can reserve the right to use them on future projects as needed. The re-use of code and other elements help us speed up our processes and work more effectively and is standard practice.

website hosting

The pricing in our proposal of services document always assumes that we will be the hosts of your project unless you requested otherwise. Our server is optimized for our workflow and we have full control of it. If you wish for us to use a different service, your project may incur an additional surcharge since it will require a change in our workflow.

If you elect to choose your own host, we cannot be held responsible for the speed, security, performance, or uptime of your site. Fixing any problems related to hosting will also be billed at our hourly rate of $32 USD. If you decide to transfer hosting - either from our hosting plan to your own, or from your own to ours - additional transfer fees may apply depending on the complexity of the transfer.

We use reasonable efforts to make sure that your site will be available 99.9% of the time during a monthly billing cycle. If we fail to meet that standard, you will be eligible to receive credit that will apply to future billing cycles based on the period of disruption. For the purposes of this agreement, disruption is defined as your site being unresponsive or returning server errors on valid user requests for more than 60 seconds of consecutive requests.

Unavailability for scheduled server maintenance is excluded from these conditions. We will announce major maintenance events in advance. Service credits must be requested within seven days of the unavailability occuring.

photography

You own the photo content produced by STS DESIGN, LLC as described above, and may use it for whatever purposes you like.

Scheduling for photo shoots will be done in advance through email, by phone, or during a project’s launch meeting.

If you require your photoshoot to be rescheduled, we will attempt to be flexible. However, please provide us with at least 24 hours notice if you require us to reschedule a photoshoot. This will allow us to adjust our schedule for maximum efficiency. Should you not be prepared upon our arrival at the scheduled location and time or provide us with notice on the same day you may be subject to a small missed meeting fee that correlates with our hourly rate of $32 USD.

non-payment & late payments

All invoices are payable upon receipt.

As mentioned in Time Frames, should a delayed payment cause a project to stall for longer than half of the estimated time frame of the project or milestone we will archive the project and require a continuation fee to resume work.

If an invoice for web hosting goes unpaid for more than 30 days after the invoice has been sent, we reserve the right to suspend your site from view until all outstanding bills are paid. We also reserve the right to charge interest of up to 12% as allowed by Washington State laws on accounts unpaid for more than 30 days.

marketing and attribution

We reserve the right to use your project as an example in our promotional material and on our website. At the closing phase of your project, we may ask for a quote describing your experience or have you fill out a short survey about your experience working with us.

We may ask you to be a reference should any future clients desire to speak with people we’ve worked with in the past.
